Welcome to the captivating world of Mahatma Gandhi's life, beautifully captured in this exclusive photo gallery. These iconic images offer glimpses into the remarkable journey of one of history's most revered figures - a leader, activist, and proponent of nonviolent resistance.

Experience the pivotal moments that shaped Gandhi's legacy, from his early years and formative experiences to his role in India's struggle for independence. Witness the historic Dandi March, a powerful symbol of nonviolent protest and a beacon of hope for a nation yearning for freedom.
